Description

Infinitarium: Close Quarters is a open world game that focuses on close combat and survival. To survive, you need to find weapons to protect yourself or attack the others. This is a multiplayer game, meaning you can be harmed not only by NPCs or the environment, but also by other players. You can parkour to reach certain places.
Inspired by Dying Light Series

Expect a lot of bugs and changes, the game is in BETA.

Features

  • Melee combat system: blocking and parrying
  • Infection level: dark zones kills yuo
  • Multiple weapons including Slashing, Blunting & Piercing
  • Multiplayer & Singleplayer
  • Day & night cycle
  • Parkour System
  • Skills Tree
